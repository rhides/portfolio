About Me

I'm a Chinese American born and raised in southern California, based in the Los Angeles area. I don't think of ineractive media as mere escapism, but as a core and personal life passion. I love learning and mastering mechanics, exploring novel worlds and ideas, and analyzing the technical trends and psychological patterns that make successful projects become part of the zeitgeist. A well-executed game shouldn't just be enjoyed, but should be studied to understand why they are appreciated.
